Smart City: Citizen Complaint & Feedback Platform

A comprehensive platform for citizens engagement in smart cities. We'll explore the challenges faced by traditional methods and how our solution addresses them through innovative technology and user-centric design. where our citizen can complain and provide feedback on public architecture, potholes, sanitation and water supply.

Key Features

Feedback System

The feedback system on our website allows citizens to report city issues, such as potholes, streetlights, and waste management, ensuring their concerns reach the right authorities.

Transparency & Security Features

Our website ensures complete transparency by allowing users to track the status of their complaints in real time. Every submitted issue is logged, categorized, and visible to relevant authorities, ensuring accountability.

Data Privacy & Compliance

Our platform prioritizes user data privacy by adhering to strict security protocols and compliance regulations. We follow legal guidelines to protect user rights, allowing safe complaint submissions without exposing sensitive information.

24x7

The website operates round the clock, allowing users to submit complaints, check updates, and access traffic or weather information anytime. Automated systems ensure that issues are logged instantly, and users receive timely updates on their concerns.

Dashboard

The dashboard provides a centralized view of all complaints, including their status (Pending, In Progress, Resolved). Users receive local notifications about complaint updates and can interact through Live Chat for quick assistance.

Live Chat

Our Live Chat feature provides instant assistance to users, ensuring a smooth experience while submitting complaints or tracking their status. Users can interact anonymously over open chat to get real-time responses to their queries.

Feedback

Our Feedback System enables users to share their experiences and rate the effectiveness of complaint resolutions. After an issue is resolved, users can provide ratings, comments, and suggestions to help improve the platformâ€™s efficiency.

Capture Image

Our platform allows users to capture and upload images or videos while submitting complaints, providing clear visual evidence of city issues. This feature ensures better accuracy and faster resolution, as authorities can assess problems directly from the uploaded media.
